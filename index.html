Our Mission

Our mission is to improve food security by educating future generations to become agriculturally self sustaining. We provide both consulting services and a modular rooftop garden system for future schools.

Teaching the Future Generation

By exposing children to sustainable practices at a young age and teaching them about the importance of nutrition, they are able to actively make decisions in support of food security in the future. The fruits and vegetables grown in the gardens will be able to give students healthy and nutritious snacks, which are crucial to a productive day.
